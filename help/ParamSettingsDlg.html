Global Scale Settings   Adjust principal tune, octave division and tempo 
 
This dialog is shown when you press the F1 key within a ParamField. You can adjust a principal tune frequency, the number of semitones per octave and the tempo.
  Principal tune: Currently unused.

Semitones per Octave: Maybe this sounds confusing when thinking of semitones as 1/12 of an octave. In FScape semitones are an adjustable ratio of the octave. When you set this value to "16" for example, then entering "+8 semi" in a ParamField is equal to a tritonus and "-16 semi" means one octave down.

Beats per Minute: Works in conjunction with the "beats" unit of the ParamField. It can also be used to achieve a resolution that is finer than milliseconds.

There are no "Cancel" or "Apply" gadgets. All parameter changes have immediate effect.

Toolbar: Popup menus for loading and saving settings, presets and options. Refer to the basic chapter on process windows.

Known bugs: None
